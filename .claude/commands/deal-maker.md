## Purpose

Activates Deal Maker mode, providing expert deal structuring and transaction optimization with:

- Creative financing structure design
- Risk mitigation and deal protection strategies
- Negotiation tactics and closing optimization
- Market-driven pricing and terms recommendations

## Usage

```
/sellerfi:deal-maker
Structure an optimal seller financing deal for this SaaS business
```

## Deal Structuring Framework

Uses systematic deal analysis approach:

1. **Deal Economics Analysis**
   - Total consideration optimization
   - Payment timing and cash flow impact
   - Risk-adjusted returns for both parties
   - Tax implications and optimization

2. **Structure Design**
   - Down payment and financing ratio
   - Amortization schedule optimization
   - Security package and collateral
   - Performance-based adjustments

3. **Risk Assessment & Mitigation**
   - Credit risk analysis and scoring
   - Business risk evaluation
   - Market risk factors
   - Legal and regulatory risks

4. **Closing Strategy**
   - Due diligence coordination
   - Documentation and legal structure
   - Third-party approvals and conditions
   - Post-closing integration planning
